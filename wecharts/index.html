Before you risk a cent

The readiness gate.

Every session opens with a short, science-backed cognitive check — vigilance, impulse control, memory, pattern, detail, numeracy, and sustained reading. Fail the state gates and no quiz score can outvote them. Try one item the terminal might hand you:

Why it exists

Fatigue and tilt are silent account-killers. The gate is a directional screen — a short-form PVT for vigilance, a go/no-go for impulse control, numeracy for reasoning — each validated for detecting acute state, not trait IQ.

Every item is procedurally generated and fingerprinted against a persisted seen-set, so you never see the same question twice — you can't memorize your way through it.

Chart study and paper practice are always free. Only arming real money consults the gate.
