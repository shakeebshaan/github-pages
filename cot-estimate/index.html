COT Nowcast — the Friday report, estimated on Wednesday

The CFTC report is a snapshot of Tuesday close that publishes Friday 15:30 ET. This estimator predicts it from market-observable data only (prices through Tuesday, previously released reports — admissibility hard-asserted), walk-forward validated over 868 weeks / 2009–2026 against named baselines. It never touches the unreleased report; estimates and official signals are separate ledgers. ← Backtest page
