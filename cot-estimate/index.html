COT Nowcast — the Friday report, estimated on Wednesday

The CFTC report is a snapshot of Tuesday close that publishes Friday 15:30 ET. This estimator predicts it from market-observable data only (prices through Tuesday, previously released reports — admissibility hard-asserted), walk-forward validated over 2009–2026 against named baselines. It never touches the unreleased report; estimates and official signals are separate ledgers. ← Backtest page

04Scorecard — ensemble vs baselines

MASE = model MAE ÷ persistence MAE (below 1 beats "no change"). B2 = the momentum-regression baseline the spec names as the falsification bar. B3 = contracts-persistence (positions held unchanged in contracts; the %OI change is then driven purely by the observed ΔOI) — it loses to plain persistence everywhere (MASE > 1 on every series), so B0 stays the MASE denominator. DM = Diebold–Mariano p-value (Newey–West). Directional accuracy counts only weeks outside the flow dead zone. Gated markets: GOLD, SILVER, COPPER, PLATINUM. STEEL-HRC is structurally skill-free (MASE ≈ 1.0 every year since 2012) and is permanently report-only — never in any gate or selection metric.

04bShadow ROI — is acting on Wednesday worth it?

Dual-entry retrospective backtest over the full walk-forward: both legs trade the identical composite → Long/Short-weights pipeline at $100k notional per leg. Leg W computes the composite from the Wednesday estimate and enters at the first real print after Wednesday noon; Leg R computes it from the official report and enters after the Friday 15:30 ET release. W − R therefore isolates the pure value of acting ~2¼ days early on the nowcast: timing edge minus prediction error. Weeks enter only when both legs are tradable and Leg W is timing-legal; disruption windows excluded. STEEL-HRC is sentiment-only (not executable) and excluded from the headline.

06Research notes — improvement queue (2026-07-10)

Nowcast accuracy. Three independent CTA-replication studies (SSRN 3674828; Invest Resolve trend-replication whitepaper; arXiv 2507.15876) recover 75–86% of trend-follower behavior from price alone — with ~98% of fitted weight on momentum lookbacks of 90+ trading days (up to 500d). This model's features currently top out at 60 days; adding vol-normalized 90/120/180/260-day momentum z-scores is the top queued upgrade, ahead of the paid daily-OI backfill (Databento CME history starts 2010, usage-priced).

Signal construction. A factor-decomposition backtest over the full 2009→2026 trade engine (see the Factor lab on the backtest page) found the composite's contrarian raw-positioning factor loses money standalone (−0.32 net Sharpe), consistent with Kang–Rouwenhorst–Tang (J. Finance 2020): positioning levels don't predict, positioning percentiles and flows do. A candidate replacement (52w-smoothed hedging pressure + 156w MM percentile) is positive net of costs in 18/18 parameter combos and with palladium excluded — now a shadow candidate, not the live signal.
