COT Nowcast — the Friday report, estimated on Wednesday

The CFTC report is a snapshot of Tuesday close that publishes Friday 15:30 ET. This estimator predicts it from market-observable data only (prices through Tuesday, previously released reports — admissibility hard-asserted), walk-forward validated over 868 weeks / 2009–2026 against named baselines. It never touches the unreleased report; estimates and official signals are separate ledgers. ← Backtest page

06Research notes — improvement queue (2026-07-10)

Nowcast accuracy. Three independent CTA-replication studies (SSRN 3674828; Invest Resolve trend-replication whitepaper; arXiv 2507.15876) recover 75–86% of trend-follower behavior from price alone — with ~98% of fitted weight on momentum lookbacks of 90+ trading days (up to 500d). This model's features currently top out at 60 days; adding vol-normalized 90/120/180/260-day momentum z-scores is the top queued upgrade, ahead of the paid daily-OI backfill (Databento CME history starts 2010, usage-priced).

Signal construction. A factor-decomposition backtest over the full 2009→2026 trade engine (see the Factor lab on the backtest page) found the composite's contrarian raw-positioning factor loses money standalone (−0.32 net Sharpe), consistent with Kang–Rouwenhorst–Tang (J. Finance 2020): positioning levels don't predict, positioning percentiles and flows do. A candidate replacement (52w-smoothed hedging pressure + 156w MM percentile) is positive net of costs in 18/18 parameter combos and with palladium excluded — now a shadow candidate, not the live signal.

07Method & guardrails

Estimates are a separate signal class with their own timestamps; the official signal's release-lag rule stays untouched. Research artifact, not financial advice.
